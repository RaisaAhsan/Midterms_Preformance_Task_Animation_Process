1.) Pick a topic of interest: Book bans. What are book bans? It's a form of censorship, it’s when organizations, governments, and individuals remove books from libraries or restrict access because they don’t approve of the content. Book bans are caused by the following groups: 45% Patrons 18% Parents 13% Board/administration 12% Political and Religious groups 8% Librarians/teachers 3% Elected officials 1% Students Where they occur: 66% Public libraries 19% School libraries 12% Schools 2% Academia 1% Special/person libraries Main causes for book bans: Racism Misinformation Graphic content Stereotypical ideas Gender Dysphoria LGBTQIA+ Profanity Radical religions Sexual content Violence
